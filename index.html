Competition Rules and Regulations

Competition Rules

  • This Yogasana Competition is open to all individuals as well as bonafide students of schools, colleges, recognized Yoga Institutions & yoga practitioners.
  • The competition will be conducted strictly as per the rules and regulations laid down by the organizers.
  • The decision of the Judges shall be final and binding in all matters.
  • Each participant must perform three compulsory asanas from the prescribed chart and two asanas of their own choice in each event.
  • Participants with serious/chronic medical conditions are advised not to participate. The organizers will not be responsible for any injury or health issues.

Dress Code

  • Male participants should wear only bottom (half shorts).
  • Female participants should wear round-neck T-shirt with tights/leggings.
  • Note: Bangles, necklaces, rings, chains, watches or any accessories should not be worn during performance.

Event Format

  • Duration: Maximum 3 minutes per event.
  • Each participant must perform:
    • 3 asanas from the given chart
    • 2 asanas of their own choice
  • Separate fee will be applicable for each event.
  • Male and Female categories will be judged separately.

Categories of Yoga Events

  • Traditional Yogasana ‚Äì Individual (Male & Female ‚Äì Separate Judging)
  • Forward Bending Asanas ‚Äì Individual (Male & Female ‚Äì Separate Judging)
  • Backward Bending Asanas ‚Äì Individual (Male & Female ‚Äì Separate Judging)
  • Twisting Asanas ‚Äì Individual (Male & Female ‚Äì Separate Judging)
  • Hand Balance Asanas ‚Äì Individual (Male & Female ‚Äì Separate Judging)

Note: Participants may register for one or more categories by paying separate entry fees for each event. In each category, participants must perform 3 compulsory asanas from the prescribed chart and 2 asanas of their own choice.

General Rules

  • Participants must report at least 30 minutes before their scheduled time.
